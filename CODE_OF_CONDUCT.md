# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to fostering an open, inclusive, and respectful community. All contributors, maintainers, and participants are expected to create a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, gender, disability, ethnicity, experience level, or background.

---

## Expected Behavior

We encourage the following behavior:

* Use respectful and inclusive language
* Be open to feedback and constructive criticism
* Respect different opinions and experiences
* Focus on collaboration and community benefit
* Show empathy and professionalism

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behavior will not be tolerated:

* Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech
* Offensive, insulting, or derogatory comments
* Personal or political attacks
* Sharing private information without permission
* Any behavior considered inappropriate in a professional environment

---

## Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct. They may take appropriate action, including warning, removing content, or banning contributors who violate these guidelines.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including repositories, issues, pull requests, discussions, and external platforms where the project is represented.

---

Violations may result in temporary or permanent actions, including warnings, removal of contributions, or banning from the project.
